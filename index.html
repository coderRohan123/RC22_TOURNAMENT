Rules

  1. THERE WILL BE 5 OVERS MATCH.
  2. THE TOURNAMENT IS DIVIDED INTO LEAGUE+PLAYOFFS STAGE
  3. ONLY 2 OVERS OF SPIN IS ALLOWED
  4. 1 YORKER AND 2 WIDE YORKER PER OVER IS ALLOWED
  5. IN CASE OF LEAVING A MATCH BETWEEN,OPPONENT WINS(REPEATEDLY LEAVING MATCHES BETWEEN WILL LEAD TO BAN FOR 2 MATCHES)
  6. AFTER FINISHING THE MATCH MAKE SURE TO POST THE SCORE CARD OF THE MATCH(OVERS PLAYED/RUNS/WICKETSLOST MUST BE VISIBLE)
  7. PLAYOFF FORMAT WILL BE OF IPL FORMAT
  8. TOP 4 TEAMS WILL QUALIFY FOR PLAYOFFS
  9. POINTS TABLE WILL BE UPDATED AFTER EACH ROUND ONLY(ALL THE MATCHES IN ROUND SHOULD BE COMPLETED)
  10. IN CASE OF DRAW Points THE NET RUN RATE/DIFF PTS WILL BE CONSIDERED
  11. THE TIME FOR COMPLETING A ROUND IS 2 DAYS IF ANY PLAYERS FAILS TO COMPLETE MATCH HE(THE PLAYER WHO WAS INACTIVE ) WILL NOT BE GIVEN POINTS
  12. IF YOU LEAVE THE GAME BETWEEN,SENSING YOU WILL LOOSE,YOU WILL BE NOT GIVEN POINTS AND REPEATEDLY MAY BAN YOU FROM THE TOURNAMENT
  13. IF FOUND OR MAJORITY PLAYERS COMPLAINT AGAINST A PARTICULAR PLAYER OF FIXING THEN HE WILL BE DISQUALIFIED IMMEDIATELY AND MAY FACE SEVERE HUMMILIATION
  14. IN CASE OF ANY DISPUTE ARISES THE FINAL DECISION WILL BE TAKEN BY THE ORGAINISER ON SUGGESTION WITH ALL THE PLAYERS
  15. THIS TOURNAMENT IS FOR ENTERTAINMENT (ABUSING,FIGHTING,QUARALLING ETC..WILL NOT BE TOLERATED) CHILL! AND ENJOY
